Subconscious Micro-behavior Analysis

Integrating psychological theories of non-conscious behaviors for authentic emotion understanding

Subconscious micro-behaviors provide authentic windows into genuine emotional states that are difficult to consciously control or manipulate. Our research integrates established psychological theories of non-conscious behaviors with advanced AI techniques to develop more accurate and psychologically-grounded emotion recognition systems.

Key Contributions

  • Identity-free Micro-gesture Recognition: Developing systems that analyze subtle behavioral cues while eliminating identity-revealing information
  • Psychological Theory Integration: Grounding AI models in established psychological theories of subconscious expression
  • Real-world Dataset Creation: Building comprehensive datasets of spontaneous micro-behaviors in naturalistic settings
  • Cross-cultural Validation: Ensuring models capture universal aspects of subconscious expression across different cultures
