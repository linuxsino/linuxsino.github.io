Remote Physiological Measurement

Non-contact Physiological Signal Analysis for Mental Health

This research focuses on non-contact physiological signal analysis through remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) and implicit emotion modeling. We develop advanced AI techniques to extract heart rate variability, respiratory patterns, and other physiological signals from video, enabling mental health monitoring and emotional state assessment without physical contact.

Demonstration of our rPPG system extracting heart rate variability and physiological signals from facial video.
