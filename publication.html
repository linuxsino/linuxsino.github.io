Research

My research focuses on the deep integration of artificial intelligence and psychological behavioral science, centered around the core theme of "Trustworthy Affective Computing." I have proposed and implemented a comprehensive psychological understanding system that spans from data collection to model construction, and from input security to output trustworthiness.
Research Vision: To develop AI systems that understand human emotions in a trustworthy, ethical, and psychologically-grounded manner, enabling applications in mental health, human-computer interaction, and public safety.

Research Impact

The integrated research framework establishes a complete trustworthy loop—from input source control through reasoning process modeling to output result correction and ethical control. This approach provides theoretical support and technical pathways for sustainable development of AI emotion recognition in critical domains including:

  • Mental Health: Objective assessment tools for psychological well-being and emotional disorders
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Ethical and psychologically-aware interactive systems
  • Public Safety: Reliable emotional state monitoring in security-critical environments
  • Healthcare: Non-contact physiological monitoring for clinical applications
