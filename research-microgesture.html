Identity-free Emotion Recognition through Micro-behaviors

Preserving privacy while maintaining accurate emotional assessment through subconscious micro-gestures

This research direction focuses on developing identity-free emotion recognition through micro-gestures and micro-movements. We pioneer non-identity micro-gesture video data systems that eliminate facial and vocal identity information, establishing new paradigms for subconscious behavioral analysis. Our work enables emotion understanding while preserving individual privacy, making it suitable for real-world applications where identity protection is crucial.
