Trustworthy Multimodal AI

Secure and Reliable Emotion Understanding in AI Systems

This research addresses critical challenges in AI emotion understanding: input authenticity, identity-independent reasoning, and emotional hallucination assessment. We develop robust verification mechanisms and multimodal frameworks that ensure AI systems provide trustworthy emotional intelligence while maintaining security and ethical standards.

Demonstration of our face anti-spoofing system detecting presentation attacks in real-time scenarios.
