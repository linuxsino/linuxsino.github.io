Biography

Dr. Xin Liu is an Associate Professor at the Computational Engineering department of LUT University. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Oulu in 2019 under the supervision of Prof. Guoying Zhao, and his Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2016 under the supervision of Prof. Chun Qi.

His research focuses on machine learning and computer vision applications for emotion AI, human behavior analysis (body gestures, actions, facial expression), and image restoration (denoising, deblurring, super-resolution).

Previously, he was an Academy of Finland postdoc fellow at the University of Oulu (working with Prof. Guoying Zhao and Prof. Matti Pietikainen), Endeavour Research Fellow at the University of Sydney (with Prof. Dacheng Tao), and visiting researcher at Imperial College London (with Prof. Stefanos Zafeiriou). His awards and grants include those from Academy of Finland, TES, Nokia Foundation, KAUTE Foundation, Otto A. Malm Foundation, Tauno Tonning Foundation, Riitta ja Jorma J. Takanen Foundation, and the Endeavour Research Fellowship of Australian Government Department of Education. Dr. Liu is a Senior Member of IEEE and member of ACM, SPIE, ELLIS, and APA.
