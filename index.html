Welcome

I am an Associate Professor at the Computational Engineering department, LUT University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Oulu (2019) and Information and Communication Engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong University (2016).

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision applications for emotion AI, human behavior analysis, and image restoration.

Formerly a postdoc at University of Oulu and visiting researcher at University of Sydney and Imperial College London. Awards include: Academy of Finland, TES grant, Nokia Foundation, Endeavour Fellowship. Senior member of IEEE, member of ACM, SPIE, ELLIS, APA.
