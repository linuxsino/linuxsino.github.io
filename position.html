Visiting Students and Scholars
Long-term Recruitment | Cross-disciplinary Welcome

We are looking for motivated visiting students and scholars to join our research on Trustworthy Affective Computing at the School of Psychology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. We welcome applicants from diverse backgrounds.

Core Research Directions:
  • Human-Machine Empathy – Developing AI systems that can understand and respond to human emotions empathetically
  • Trustworthy Affective Computing – Building reliable, ethical, and psychologically-consistent emotion AI
  • Multimodal Large Language Models for Emotion – Advancing emotion understanding through MLLMs

Preferred Research Experience

Candidates with research experience in the following areas will be given priority:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) and Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)
  • Micro-gesture and Micro-expression Analysis
  • Facial Action Unit Detection
  • Remote Physiological Measurement
  • Multimodal Emotion Understanding
  • AI Ethics and Psychological Consistency

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD program
  • Strong background in related fields
  • Programming experience (Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc.)
  • Good English communication skills
  • Self-motivated and passionate about research

Benefits

  • Work on cutting-edge affective computing research
  • Access to advanced computing facilities
  • Collaboration with leading researchers
  • Potential for co-authorship in publications
  • Flexible duration (3-12 months)

How to Apply

Send the following documents to xin.liu@sjtu.edu.cn:

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Academic transcripts
  • Brief statement of research interests
  • Expected duration of visit
  • Any relevant research experience

We review applications on a rolling basis.
