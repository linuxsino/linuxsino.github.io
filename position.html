Visiting Students and Scholars
Long-term Recruitment | Cross-disciplinary Welcome

Our research group is actively seeking motivated visiting students and scholars to join our dynamic team working on Trustworthy Affective Computing. We welcome applicants from diverse academic backgrounds to collaborate on cutting-edge research projects.

Trustworthy Affective Computing Framework

1

Input Authenticity

Liveness detection, anti-spoofing, physiological signal verification

2

Causal Reasoning

Psychology-guided analysis, micro-behavior understanding, action unit mapping

3

Trustworthy Output

Ethical constraints, hallucination detection, psychological consistency

Research Focus Areas

  • Trustworthy Affective Computing: From identity-dependent to de-identified emotion recognition
  • Micro-gesture and Micro-expression Analysis: Subconscious behavior understanding
  • Facial Action Unit Detection: Psychology-constrained emotion mapping
  • Remote Physiological Measurement: Contactless vital sign monitoring
  • Multimodal Emotion Understanding: Integrating visual, textual, and physiological signals
  • AI Ethics and Psychological Consistency: Ensuring trustworthy emotional AI

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD program
  • Strong academic background in related fields
  • Programming experience (Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc.)
  • Good communication skills in English
  • Self-motivated and passionate about trustworthy AI research

Benefits

  • Work on cutting-edge trustworthy affective computing research
  • Access to state-of-the-art computing facilities and datasets
  • Opportunity to collaborate with leading researchers in affective computing
  • Potential for co-authorship in high-impact publications
  • Flexible duration (3-12 months)

How to Apply

Interested candidates should send the following documents to linuxsino@gmail.com:

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Academic transcripts
  • Brief statement of research interests
  • Expected duration of visit
  • Any relevant research experience or publications

We review applications on a rolling basis and welcome inquiries at any time.

主要研究方向

  • 可信情感计算：从身份依赖到去身份化的情感识别
  • 微手势与微表情分析：潜意识行为理解
  • 面部动作单元检测：心理学约束的情感映射
  • 远程生理测量：非接触式生命体征监测
  • 多模态情感理解：整合视觉、文本和生理信号
  • AI伦理与心理一致性：确保可信的情感AI
