Related Fields

Research Areas

  • Trustworthy Affective Computing
  • Micro-gesture and Micro-expression Analysis
  • Facial Action Unit Detection
  • Remote Physiological Measurement
  • Multimodal Emotion Understanding
  • AI Ethics and Psychological Consistency

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD program
  • Strong background in related fields
  • Programming experience (Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc.)
  • Good English communication skills
  • Self-motivated and passionate about research

Benefits

  • Work on cutting-edge affective computing research
  • Access to advanced computing facilities
  • Collaboration with leading researchers
  • Potential for co-authorship in publications
  • Flexible duration (3-12 months)

How to Apply

Send the following documents to linuxsino@gmail.com:

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Academic transcripts
  • Brief statement of research interests
  • Expected duration of visit
  • Any relevant research experience

We review applications on a rolling basis.
