Research

My research at the intersection of Psychology and Artificial Intelligence focuses on developing trustworthy affective computing systems that understand human emotions in psychologically-grounded and ethically-aware ways. By combining insights from psychological science with advanced AI techniques, we aim to create emotion AI systems that are not only technically advanced but also psychologically valid and socially responsible.

Bridging Psychology and AI for Trustworthy Emotion Understanding

Developing psychologically-informed AI systems that understand human emotions while preserving privacy, ensuring security, and maintaining ethical standards.

Research Philosophy

Psychology-AI Integration: We believe that truly effective emotion AI must be grounded in psychological science. Our research integrates theories from cognitive psychology, psychophysiology, and behavioral science with state-of-the-art AI techniques.

Our research follows a principled approach that emphasizes:

  • Psychological Validity: Ensuring our models align with established psychological theories and empirical findings
  • Technical Innovation: Developing novel AI algorithms that address real challenges in emotion understanding
  • Ethical Considerations: Prioritizing privacy, fairness, and transparency in all our systems
  • Real-world Impact: Creating technologies that can be practically deployed in mental health, education, and human-computer interaction

Research Impact

Our work establishes a complete trustworthy loop—from input source control through psychologically-informed reasoning to output result correction and ethical control. This integrated approach provides theoretical support and technical pathways for sustainable development of AI emotion recognition in critical domains including:

Mental Health

Objective assessment tools for psychological well-being, stress detection, and emotional disorders through non-contact monitoring

Human-Computer Interaction

Ethical and psychologically-aware interactive systems with privacy-preserving emotion understanding

Education & Learning

Adaptive learning systems responsive to student emotional states and engagement levels

Public Safety

Reliable emotional state monitoring in security-critical environments while preserving individual privacy
